# **App Name**: LeafLux's Tea Finder

## Core Features:

- Nearby Tea Shop Discovery: Utilize user's current location to identify and display the closest authentic tea shops within a user-configurable radius, expanding search range as needed.
- Authenticity Filtering: Allow users to filter search results to include only shops selling loose leaf tea, teaware, or offering tea classes, explicitly excluding coffee shops and bubble tea establishments.
- Ethical Sourcing Filter: Allow users to filter search results based on ethical sourcing and fair trade practices of tea shops.
- Detailed Shop Profiles: View comprehensive information for each tea shop, including address, offerings (loose leaf, teaware, classes), contact details, and external links.
- Map Integration: Display tea shop locations on an interactive map for easy visual navigation and route planning.
- Tea Data Repository: Maintain and manage a curated database of authentic tea shops, including their location, services, and qualifying features.
- Personalized Tea Recommendations Tool: A generative AI tool that suggests unique tea types, brewing methods, or local tea events based on user preferences and the characteristics of nearby authentic tea shops.
- Waitlist Sign-Up: Allow users to sign up for a waitlist for future features and updates.
- Contact Section: Provide a contact section for users to submit feedback and inquiries.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Soft, muted sage green (#A8B893) to evoke a sense of natural tranquility and sophistication, reminiscent of tea fields and traditional tea ceremonies.
- Background color: Light beige (#F5F5DC) to provide a warm, inviting canvas that complements the sage green and creates a sense of calm elegance.
- Accent color: Subtle bronze (#B87333) for interactive elements and highlights, adding a touch of understated luxury and visual interest without overpowering the serene palette.
- Headline font: 'Lora' (serif), chosen for its refined and readable elegance, enhancing the sophisticated and thoughtful aesthetic. Body text font: 'Open Sans' (sans-serif), providing a modern, objective, and highly readable experience for conveying detailed information about tea shops.
- Use refined line-art icons that subtly incorporate tea-related motifs like tea leaves, teacups, or kettles, rendered in a style that complements the elegant and whimsical theme without being overly ornate.
- Implement a clean, spacious layout with ample whitespace to foster a sense of calm and clarity. Prioritize an intuitive information hierarchy to make shop discovery and details easily accessible, akin to a meticulously curated art exhibition.
- Incorporate subtle, flowing animations for transitions and interactive elements, such as a gentle fade-in for shop details or a soft ripple effect on map markers, adding a touch of whimsy and fluid elegance without distraction.
